This website is designed for GITAM Students to have a glimpse about their carreer opportunities and also connect to their Alumni.



  • GITAM Students can see your Alumni details, their status and you can also connect with them through their linkedin Id for any queries regarding career and job experience.

  • GITAM Students do have the dilemma or confusion related to internships. The website provides different internship opportunities available for ENGINEERING, SCIENCE, MANAGEMENT and MEDICAL. The site also gives info about the skills required to work with such internship program.

  • GITAM Students can also seek information about which all companies are visiting on campus for recruitment, their average packages, and on which month the company visits.

  • GITAM Students faces a lot issues regarding information conveyed to them through mails. Day to day students receive a lot of necessary and unnecessary mails on their email accounts. This website also has a notification panel where all important information is given in the summarized manner along with a link associated with it.
